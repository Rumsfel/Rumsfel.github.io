Did You Know?

The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is over 13,000 miles long and took more than 2,000 years to build.

Lake Baikal

Lake Baikal in Siberia is the deepest lake in the world, reaching depths of over 5,300 feet.

The Sahara Desert

The Sahara Desert expands at about 0.6 miles per year due to desertification.

Amazon Rainforest

The Amazon Rainforest produces 20% of the Earth's oxygen and is home to one in ten known species in the world.

Mount Everest

Mount Everest, the highest mountain in the world, stands at 29,029 feet above sea level.
